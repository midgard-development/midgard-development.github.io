Bes Music

Since OneDrive no longer Supports streaming, and Groove killed downloading of music and syncing playlistst the same time, I needed an alternative for Windows 10 Mobile and Win 10. And so Bes Music was created. It downloads your Music from OneDrive Syncing your Playlists and mimic the old Zune player a little bit.

I prefere having my Music always with me so it currently does not support streaming, but will download your music from OneDrive in advance.

It will need the latest Windows 10 Mobile Version. It ist Testet on an Lumia 950 DualSim and Win 10 1903.
